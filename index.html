About Me:

I was an American business magnate, industrial designer, media proprietor and investor. I was a charismatic pioneer of the personal computer era. With Steve Wozniak, Jobs founded Apple Inc. in 1976 and transformed the company into a world leader in telecommunications. It is one of the best- regarded brands globally. What it means, is that Apple is a premium brand masquerading as a luxury one. Gold dipping a product that will be outdated because of its technology in a year is just a waste of gold. The price point of Apple Watches is more suited to the aspirational affluent purchaser.

The Santa Clara county, south of the Bay Area, became known as Silicon Valley in the early 1950s after the sprouting of myriads of semi-conductor companies in the area. As a result, young Steve Jobs grew up in a neighborhood filled with engineers working on electronics and other gizmos in their garages on weekends. This shaped his interest in the field as he grew up.
